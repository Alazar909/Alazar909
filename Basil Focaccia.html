Basil Focaccia

what is Basil Focaccia

it is an Italian flatbread made from a base similar to pizza dough that can be either sweet or savoury.

This is my mother-in-law's recipe. It's easy and delicious. You can make it with an olive oil topping or tomato sauce topping.
